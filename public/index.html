How mascot-gen works.

A controlled mascot generator for turning rough character ideas into brand-safe unit prompts, then editing the selected source mascot with FAL Nano Banana 2.

Pipeline

01

Source

Pick a mascot image. The source preserves pose, camera, material quality, and core proportions.

Brief

Prompt stack

What goes into the final prompt

  • Preservation rules: keep pose, camera, proportions, and rendering style.
  • Brand color rules: only colors from DESIGN.md.
  • Creative concept: title, archetype, story, visual principle, signatures, avoid rules.
  • 9 part prompts: editable physical controls for the mascot body.
  • Settings: aspect ratio, resolution, format, thinking, safety.
Artifact cleanup

What is not identity

Glasses, lenses, flowers, ties, collars, shirts, plaid cloth, backpacks, novelty props, snack packs, phones, smoking-related packs, and third-party logos are source artifacts. If the concept does not ask for them, the prompt removes, neutralizes, or converts them into brand-safe modules.

Settings

Generation controls

Nano Banana 2 edit accepts aspect ratio, output format, safety tolerance, thinking level, and resolution. The UI exposes the common controls and keeps conservative defaults for mascot consistency.
